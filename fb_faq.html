Facebook Education Guide for CCSA

“Sharing is Caring”

What is Facebook?
Facebook began as one college student’s attempt to recreate the social side of college online. Within a few days, the first Facebook grew. Today the online social network is probably the largest group of Internet users online.

Why should I use Facebook as a member of CCSA?
We believe Facebook is a great place to share sailing with family, friends, members of the community and future members.

How do I sign up? (link - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CtuUzZRBN0Y)
Signing up is pretty easy. Go to Facebook.com (link - https://www.facebook.com/) and follow the instructions. Remember, you decide what you choose to share and choose not to share.
