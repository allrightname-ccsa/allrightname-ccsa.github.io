Join our Yahoo! Group:

CCSA members use our Yahoo Group as a main means of communication. You can:

  • Send an email to all CCSA members in The Yahoo Group.
  • Receive messages from the group about upcoming events.
  • Review important files.
  • Look at photo albums.
  • Check out sailing “Links”.

To join the CCSA Yahoo Group:

  1. Click on the link below which will take you to our group’s page in Yahoo.
  2. Once you are on Yahoo, click on the blue, “Join This Group” button just above our photo.
  3. If you are already have a Yahoo ID and password just sign in and go to the CCSA Group.
  4. If you don’t have a Yahoo ID, click on the “Sign Up” link and then follow the instructions. After you sign up, go to the group page and then bookmark it in your browser’s “Favorites”.
