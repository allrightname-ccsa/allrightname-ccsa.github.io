What is Facebook?
What does it do for CCSA?

Facebook began as one college student’s attempt to recreate the social side of college online. Within a few days, the first Facebook grew. Today the online social network is probably the largest group of Internet users online. His story was told in the movie "The Social Network". Watch the trailer below or keep scrolling for more about CCSA on Facebook.

 

 

 

We believe Facebook is a great place to share sailing with family, friends, members of the community and future members.

 

On the web it is often said "Sharing is Caring" becasue the more you share our club by "Liking" and clicking "Share" , the your friends will be exposed to what we do as a club.

 

Imagine going for a sail at the lake. You take a picture and "post" it on your Facebook timeline. All of your 100 friends see the photo and think, "cool" sailing at Creve Coeur Lake. Five of your friends share the "post" with their friends. Each of their friends has 100 freinds. Now you have reached 600 people and maybe one of them had always wanted to learn how to sail. That is how Facebook can work for the club.
