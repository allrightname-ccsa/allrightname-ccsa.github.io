Sailing Video Library
Welcome to our video collection. Through the years sailors have wanted to describe sailing to land lovers, a difficult task to be sure. So much to say: the pull of the sea, invitation for adventure, exploring lands not found yet be Europeans, racing against another skipper, sailing around the world alone. This is a collection of some of our favorites. They offer a glimpse into the history and tradition of sailing.
 

Sailing Movies We Like
"Wind" - 1992 - Fictional story based on the historic 1983 America's Cup loss to Austrailia. "The only thing better than winning the America's Cup, is losing it and getting it back." (Most of the club can quote the movie). Watch this either before or after "America's Cup 1983 (Austrailian Story"
"Master & Commander, The Far Side of the World" - 2003 - A British boat captain is sent to "Sink, Burn, or take her a Prize" one of the newest American built French Man of War; The Acheron. The film describes a time when these boats were the only way to get around the world. Does this captain go too far as he sacrafices his crew for King & Country?
"Morning Light" - What happens when you tunn over the keys to a Volvo Ocean Racing boat to a bunch of high school and college studetns? Disney aks this in their own documentary. They invite, select and train youth to match witts and blisters against the world's best skippers in a race from LA to Hawaii.
"Dove" - 1970's movie about a 17 year old who famously sailing alone around the world.
 

Great Sailing Documentaries and Racing Films on Youtube
"To the Ends of the Earth: First 40 Years I Volvo Ocean Race" - It will give you chills. Film describing the history of the around the world race. (Watch this and then watch the next film about the 2011-2012 race.)
Volvo Race Documentary 2011-2012 Race - A Documentary about the 2011-2012 around the world Volvo Ocean Race. (Spoiler: A boat breaks a mast!)
"America’s Cup 1983 (Australian Story)" - The 52-minute documentary describes the first America's Cup Race in over 100 years where the American's lose the cup told by the Austrainains who won it. Amazing! (Spoiler: The Aussies Win)
The Volvo Ocean Race Channel: If you are into binge watching, binge watch these! The ocean race decided to make their own channel online severl years ago. During the race, they broadcast live on their YouTube Channel and post the videos after the race has been completed.
