We use the two boat ramps on the hillside of the park. On race days our race committee sets up a tent next to Marine Ave and the two boat ramps.

Driving directions: I-270 to Dorsett Rd, West on Dorsett Rd for about a mile, right on Marine Av, down the hill and you'll find us on the first parkinglot on the left. There is usually parking across Marine Ave.
Boat Trailer Parking: First come first serve. It's a popular park and it gets busy on great sailing days.
